Blog 5 - Reflection on Emotional Intelligence

  • What learnings did you take from the chapter?
    • 5 domains of emotional intelligence: (1) Self-awareness; (2) Self-regulation; (3) Motivation; (4) Empathy; (5) Social Skills
    • (1)-(3)= intrapersonal; (4)-(5)= interpersonal
    • Top 6 competencies that distinguish tech sector performers, majority of which are EQ: (1) Achievement drive and standard; (2) Ability to influence; (3) Conceptual thinking; (4) Analytic ability; (5) Initiative; (6) Self-confience
    • Attention is a key foundation to emotional intelligence
    • Mindfulness= paying attention in a particular way: on purpose, in the present moment, and non-judgementally
    • Affect labeling (=labeling a feeling) helps you manage that emotion
    • Focusing an emotion to the body enables high-resolution perception= you can watch the subtle changes of the emotion as it waxes, wanes, and ceases
  • How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

    • I'm not much into meditation. I decided to try it, but I think I prefer to find mindfulness in ways other than just sitting and focusing on my breathing: going on walks, for example, is a really enjoyable way for me to spend time with myself and introspect.
